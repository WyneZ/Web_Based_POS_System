## ğŸ” Detailed Data Flow Per Role

---

1. Cashier

#### Actions:

* Scans product â†’ fetches from `/products/`
* Adds to cart â†’ frontend handles logic
* Finalizes sale â†’ POST to `/sales/`
* Submits payment â†’ `/sales/<id>/payment/`

#### Data Flow:

1. Sends sale details to **Sales API**
2. Sales API:

   * Records sale + items in `Sale` and `SaleItem`
   * Deducts stock via **StockTransaction**
3. Response includes receipt

---

2. Manager

#### Actions:

* Views dashboard analytics â†’ `/reports/*`
* Adds/updates products â†’ `/products/`
* Manages users â†’ `/users/`
* Adjusts pricing, categories

#### Data Flow:

1. Fetches data from `reports/`, `sales/`, `products/`
2. Posts/updates to `products/` or `users/` APIs
3. May approve large stock changes or returns

---

3. Inventory Staff

#### Actions:

* Adds stock â†’ `/stock/transactions/` with positive quantity
* Records damaged/expired stock â†’ negative quantity
* Manages suppliers â†’ `/suppliers/`

#### Data Flow:

1. Posts stock movement
2. System logs it as `StockTransaction`
3. Updates `Product.stock_quantity`
